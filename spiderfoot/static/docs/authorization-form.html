4. Scope of Service

BotStop performs passive OSINT reconnaissance only. All scanning is conducted using publicly available data sources, open-source intelligence techniques, and non-intrusive queries. BotStop does not conduct penetration testing, exploit vulnerabilities, access private systems, or attempt to bypass authentication.

Findings in the resulting report reflect what is already publicly visible or discoverable by any third party, including potential attackers, without any special access or credentials.

Client acknowledges that BotStop is authorized to:

  • Query public DNS records, WHOIS databases, and certificate transparency logs for in-scope domains
  • Check in-scope domains and associated email addresses against public breach and threat intelligence databases
  • Analyze publicly accessible web pages, headers, and metadata for the in-scope assets listed above
  • Search publicly indexed sources (search engines, code repositories, paste sites) for references to in-scope domains and associated assets
  • Deliver a written report of findings, including exposure details, severity ratings, and remediation recommendations, to the authorized contact listed above
  • Retain scan data for up to 90 days for report generation and follow-up scans, after which data will be deleted upon request
Explicitly Out of Scope — BotStop Will Not:
  • Attempt to exploit, access, or extract data from any system, database, or application
  • Conduct social engineering, phishing simulations, or physical security testing
  • Scan, probe, or query any domain, IP, or asset not listed in Section 2 of this form
  • Access, download, or store any private, confidential, or personally identifiable information discovered during scanning
  • Share, publish, or disclose findings to any third party without explicit written consent from the client
  • Provide legal compliance certifications (SOC 2, HIPAA, PCI-DSS, GDPR) — this is an exposure report, not a compliance audit
5. Confidentiality & Data Handling

BotStop treats all client data, scan results, and report contents as strictly confidential. Findings will only be shared with the authorized contact listed in Section 1 unless the client provides written authorization to share with additional parties.

Scan data is stored on secured infrastructure. Client may request deletion of all associated data at any time by emailing the address listed on this form. BotStop will confirm deletion within 5 business days.
